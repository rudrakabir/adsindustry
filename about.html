About ADS Industry

Following on PVS International Industries illustrious legacy in the metal, plastics and diamond abrasive sector, ADS Industries was established to offer discerning customers high quality products in the plastic manufacturing sector.

In the face of stiff competition from Chinese made goods, ADS has thrived in the market, providing indigenously manufactured plastic goods across categories like Dispenser Pumps, PET and HDPE Bottles, Power Tools Industry Products. A renowned manufacturer of injection molded parts and products, all of our products are proudly Made In India.
